About Us

Latta Clothing is a premier Nepali fashion brand, committed to delivering quality, sustainable, and stylish apparel for men and women. We blend traditional craftsmanship with modern designs, creating timeless pieces that celebrate Nepali culture and global trends alike.

Our mission is to bring you comfortable, eco-friendly fashion at affordable prices while supporting local artisans and sustainable practices.

At Latta Clothing, we believe that fashion is an expression of identity and culture. Through our collections, we aim to connect heritage with contemporary styles, empowering you to make a statement that is both meaningful and fashionable.
